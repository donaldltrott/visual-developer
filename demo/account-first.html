How do my Customers make their payments?

Part of your BATON Merchant Service is a BATON Account.

Your BATON Account:

  • Is in your business name
  • Contains a uniquely assigned bank account
  • Contains a local P.O. Box address

Your Customers continue to make payments in your business name, and payments are sent to your BATON Account. You are required to include your BATON Account as the remittance information on all invoices processed through BATON. Invoices submitted without your BATON Account as the remittance information will be declined for payment.
