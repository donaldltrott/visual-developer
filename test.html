Understanding Open Invoices

Open invoices are any invoice that you, the Merchant, has received payment on, but the invoice payment from the Customer is still pending.

Open invoices will include the Customer(s):

  • Unpaid Invoices
  • Disputed Invoices
  • Unsettled Invoices

Disputed Invoices are invoices for which BATON has been notified of a payment dispute.

Unsettled Invoices are invoices that have been partially paid by the Customer but a balance remains.

Note: If you have selected a date range in the CUSTOMER ACTIVITY REPORTS, the Open Invoice Activity summarized in this section reflects Open Invoices as of the last day of the date range selected.

Details on the Open Invoices summarized in this chart can be seen in the Invoice Level Activity Detail reports section.
